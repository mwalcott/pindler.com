4515-Crofton is a drapery sheer linen stripe. Crofton is a transitional semisheer that is woven in a linen, viscose rayon, cotton, and polyester blend. Crofton is a classic balanced stripe pattern with a plain weave sheer ground with herringbone texture stripes alternating with pinstripes. This fabric is also well priced, making it an easy answer for a variety of room schemes.

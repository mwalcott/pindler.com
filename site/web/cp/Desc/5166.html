5166-Thatch is a multipurpose solid. Thatch is a textural solid that has a tweed look. Thatch is a transitional texture that has been designed with a random strie effect using a space dyed yarn. The strie texture is an inherent characteristic of the fabric and should not be considered an imperfection. Thatch is suitable for a wide range of looks, including mid-century modern, traditional, and transitional.

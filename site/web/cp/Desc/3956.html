3956-Abruzzo is a Signature Exclusive upholstery epingle damask. An epingle is a type of loop pile fabric woven on a wire loom or epingle loom. The art of epingle weaving originated in Lucca, Italy. Abruzzo is an exquisite epingle design with a refined wool loop pile and a subtle, lustrous fill yarn forming a classic damask pattern in the background. The Platinum Collection defines the essence of true quality from fiber to fabric incorporating classic design to the height of contemporary styling. The Platinum Collection is a fabulous grouping of epingles, cut velvets, damasks, luxurious solids, and casement draperies woven in the finest wools, linens, rayons from Germany.

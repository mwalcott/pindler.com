1981-Surat is a drapery sheer FR design. Surat is a beautiful design in 100% polyester that resembles a silk tussah drapery cloth. Surat is a new version of our existing pattern 2883-Sansilk, which has been a classic for us for a number of years. Sansilk only comes in 48" widths, but Surat will be offered in 54" widths. Surat features a variegated texture with occasional slubs that gives it a natural look.

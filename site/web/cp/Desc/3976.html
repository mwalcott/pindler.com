3976-Mikhail is a Signature Exclusive upholstery small repeat. Mikhail is a classic interlocking geometric basketweave design that can function as a small scale pattern or as a textural plain. Mikhail is woven with a matte mini shoelace yarn in the fill creating an interesting surface dimension and texture. Made in America using the finest yarns and fibers, this is a fabulous grouping of luxurious upholstery designs colored in sophisticated, modern metal neutrals accented with trend pop colors.

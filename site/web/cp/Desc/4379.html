4379-Bainbridge is a Signature Exclusive contemporary upholstery chenille stripe. Bainbridge is a textural horizontal stripe that incorporates several different colors of chenille and novelty yarns for an interesting blend of texture and color. Weave effects have also been engineered to give this pattern a unique and handwoven look. Luxury Textures are woven with the finest quality and innovative yarns spun from bamboos, cottons, linens, rayons which are woven into plush chenilles.

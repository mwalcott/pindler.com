2671-Simpatico is a Signature Exclusive upholstery contemporary damask design. Simpatico is a large-scale stylized Suzani. Simpatico is a beautiful design of a stylized organic vine silhouette, woven with a lustrous rayon twisted yarn, framing two colors of Suzani medallions. The pattern is accented with a luxuriously textural ground mixing novelty rayon, polyester, cotton, and a bit of flax and is finished with Teflon.

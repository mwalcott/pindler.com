1480-Mimet is a Signature Exclusive upholstery ethnic stripe design. Inspired by classic southern Spanish Talavera tiles, Mimet is a textural midscale ethnic jacquard design accented with an interesting ombree stripe. Constructed with a subtle, lustrous rayon blend, Mimet is best suited for upholstery use. Mimet has been treated with Teflon for added wearability and versatility.

4314-Auvent is a Signature Exclusive Richard Frinier indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® upholstery stripe. Auvent is an indoor/outdoor textural railroaded balanced stripe that has the look of French grain sacks. Auvent is woven in a blend of Sunbrella solution dyed all-weather acrylic and recycled Sunbrella acrylic. These Signature Exclusive patterns are engineered with variances in the weave for a handwoven, organic look.

3811-Ambrose is a Signature Exclusive upholstery ethnic design. Ambrose is a traditional rug-like geometric ikat pattern made modern by the use of interesting color combinations. Woven with strie chenille in the background, Ambrose an ikat look and has several different weave textures within the design. Ambrose incorporates classic design elements such as paisleys and medallions along with transitional diamond and geometric shapes.

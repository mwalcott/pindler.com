4555-Lancer is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® herringbone. Lancer is a classic herringbone pattern that emulates traditional menswear. Lancer is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

3540-Haddington is a multipurpose solid design. Haddington is a classic cotton and linen blend solid in a plain weave. Haddington is woven with unbleached linen yarns in the warp, which give the fabric a unique and natural texture and charm. The cotton yarns contain "motes" which are intentional. Motes are small flecks from the cotton seeds, left in the yarn to achieve a rustic look.

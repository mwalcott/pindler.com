4669-Asprey is an Exclusive drapery silk blend damask for drapery and bedding. Asprey is a whimsical and colorful graphic pattern. Asprey has a myriad of stylized flowers, paisleys, scrolls, and medallions arranged in a vertical formation. Asprey is woven in silk, viscose rayon, cotton, and polyester and suitable for drapery, decorative bedding, accessories, and pillows.

3701-Bella Vite is a Signature Exclusive Newport Mansions Doris Duke design. This design was inspired by an 18th century crewel embroidery found in the textile archives at Doris Duke’s Newport Estate, Rough Point. The document was originally applied to a settee, and later removed to preserve it in the archives. The original piece was embroidered with wool using French knots, and a couching technique. Our interpretation is using a linen ground with cotton embroidery. These collections are a fantastic assortment of fabrics that reflect the timeless beauty and lavish elegance that the Newport Mansions™ has come to represent.

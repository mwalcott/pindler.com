1481-Devonia is a Signature Exclusive upholstery damask design. Devonia is a contemporary modern floral damask with silhouettes of flowers against a linen woven background. Devonia incorporates several interesting weave textures that give the pattern depth and dimension. Devonia is beautifully constructed with rayon blend, given added texture with a natural linen yarn in the ground weave. Devonia is versatile as well as durable and is finished with Teflon.

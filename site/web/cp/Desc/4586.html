4586-Evetta is a multipurpose linen stripe. Evetta is a classic up the roll smaller scale balanced stripe that has the French Laundry look and is woven in 100% Belgian linen. Belgian Linen is known for its superior quality due to centuries-old farming, spinning, weaving and finishing techniques. Belgian linen is grown and produced in the best climatic regions for flax and linen yarns. Evetta has a herringbone weave in the background for added texture and surface dimension, and is washed to make the fabric softer and more casual looking. Evetta has a great hand and drape.

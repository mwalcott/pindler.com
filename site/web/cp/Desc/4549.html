4549-Relic is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® midscale pattern. Relic is an interesting hieroglyphics pattern, with different elements scattered across a plain weave ground. Relic is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

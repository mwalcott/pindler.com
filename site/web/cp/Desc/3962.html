3962-Muse is a Signature Exclusive sheer drapery pattern. Muse is a modern sheer casement that is woven on a linen lino warp. Muse features a spherical ikat silhouette design which is woven into the fabric, and then clipped using a fil coupe technique. Muse is woven in Germany. The Platinum Collection defines the essence of true quality from iber to fabric incorporating classic design to the height of contemporary styling. The Platinum Collection is a fabulous grouping of epingles, cut velvets, damasks, luxurious solids, and casement draperies woven in the finest wools, linens, rayons from Germany.

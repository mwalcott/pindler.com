5454-Supple is an upholstery vinyl solid. Supple is a classic and versatile faux leather that is soft and pliable, and easy to upholster with. Supple is specially formulated to make it remarkably easy to clean, passes high hydrolysis testing, and is cold crack resistant. Comfortable and soft, Supple lives up to its name as a supple faux leather texture that adds an element of luxury to any setting.

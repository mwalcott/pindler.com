1878-Coggeshall is a Signature Exclusive Newport Mansions Indoor/Outdoor upholstery design. Coggeshall was inspired by a traditional Pasisir batik cloth found in the archives of The Preservation Society of Newport County. The "cocohan" technique of small dots in the ground, which is exclusive to Indramayu Java, has been carefully captured in our exclusive design. Coggeshall has been woven with Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all-weather acrylic yarns. Our Sunbrella collection captures the casual, yet lavish lifestyle of summering in Newport, which is considered one of the most historic areas in American history.

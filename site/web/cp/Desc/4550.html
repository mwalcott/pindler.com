4550-Papago is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® midscale pattern. Papago is an ikat diamond with alternating figure colors in a half drop that reads like a small scale design. Papago is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

5409-Piero is an Exclusive drapery stripe. Piero is a horizontal semi sheer stripe woven on a lino warp, with matte slub yarns in the ground accented with luster novelty cord yarns in the stripes. The stripes of Piero have colors that alternate and compliment the ground for a decorative look. Piero is an interesting horizontal stripe that has a contrast of matte and luster yarns. Piero is a modern drapery that is woven in Germany.

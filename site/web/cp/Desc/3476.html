3476-Lanka is a Signature Exclusive drapery 100% raw silk sheer casement. Lanka is a gorgeous sheer casement that is woven in India from 100% Fesua silk. This beautiful hand spun yarn is one of the heaviest raw silk yarns and can only be woven on handlooms. Variations in weave and texture are what give this fabric its unique characteristics and charm. Lanka has a beautiful hand and drape, and is a fabulous textural accent for any drapery application.

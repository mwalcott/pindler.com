4561-Tesori is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® midscale pattern. Tesori is a transitional small medallion pattern with an ethnic look, with a textural background. Tesori is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

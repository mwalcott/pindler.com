4545-Oslo is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® midscale geometric. Oslo is a modern take on an enlarged basketweave pattern, with bright colors in a plain weave contrasting with a neutral toned rep weave. Oslo is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

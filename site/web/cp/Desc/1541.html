1541-Wells is an upholstery solid wool flannel design. Wells is a beautiful wool, polyester and viscose rayon blend and polyester blend velvet. Wells is suitable for a wide variety of applications as it is durable enough for upholstery but soft and drapeable enough for bedding, accessories, or at the window. This gorgeous wool flannel solid has been woven to emulate a felt texture with a softened brushed finish.

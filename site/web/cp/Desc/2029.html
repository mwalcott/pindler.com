2029-Bokhara is an Signature Exclusive upholstery contemporary damask design. Bokhara is a stylized vine and foliage silhouette design with an Eastern influence. It is a graphic pattern with a textural background accented and a bold, transitional allover vine pattern. Bokhara features an interesting mix of rayon, cotton, flax, and polyester, for a varied play on texture and luster. Bokhara is durable as well, and has been finished with Teflon for added strength and durability.

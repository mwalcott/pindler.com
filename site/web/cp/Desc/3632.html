3632-Cableknit is an exclusive upholstery stripe design. Cableknit is a midscale stripe design that can be used in a traditional or eclectic modern sense. Cableknit is updated with fresh, current colors and is woven in a textural cotton, rayon, polyester, acrylic, and flax blend and is finished with Teflon. The patterns in Gentlemen’s Retreat are exclusive to Pindler & Pindler and have been colored in classic sophisticated men’s suiting palettes paired with eye catching accent pop colors.

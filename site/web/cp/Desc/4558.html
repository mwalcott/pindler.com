4558-Tiburon is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® small scale pattern. Tiburon is a transitional all over diamond pattern with an ethnic look to it. Tiburon is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

4584-Leland is a multipurpose linen stripe. Leland is a classic up the roll balanced stripe that has the French Laundry look and is woven in 100% Belgian linen. Belgian Linen is known for its superior quality due to centuries-old farming, spinning, weaving and finishing techniques. Belgian linen is grown and produced in the best climatic regions for flax and linen yarns. Leland is washed to make the fabric softer and more casual looking. Leland has a great hand and drape.

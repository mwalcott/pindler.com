4699-Florentina is an Exclusive Newport Mansions Doris Duke™ multipurpose large scale floral embroidery. Florentina was inspired by a circa 1925 yellow silk day dress found in the wardrobe archives at Doris Duke’s Newport Estate, Rough Point. Our version of Florentina is an exotic mutlicolored floral and leaf vine pattern embroidered on a linen blend ground. The Newport Mansions Doris Duke™ Indoor/Outdoor Collection is an eclectic assortment of exotic patterns inspired by the personal gatherings of an iconic world traveler, Doris Duke.

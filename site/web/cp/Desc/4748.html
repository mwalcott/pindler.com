4548-Marava is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® large scale pattern. Marava is transitional ikat design with fresh colors in an the figure against a neutral toned background. Marava is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

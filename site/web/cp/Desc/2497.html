2497-Pinebrook is an upholstery solid velvet design. Pinebrook is a wide wale 1/2" velvet corduroy pattern that is woven in 100% polyester microfiber that is incredibly soft and comfortable. Due to the nature of the fabric, the plush pile will brush up in different directions, causing a variegated texture on the surface. This characteristic is intended to add to the visual interest of the fabric.

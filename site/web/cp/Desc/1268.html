1268-Prestige is a drapery solid design. Prestige is a drapery solid faux silk design woven beautifully to emulate a lustrous raw silk look. Prestige is a combination of earthy texture, sumptuousness and sophistication. Highs and lows in the yarns coupled with fine lustrous yarns give Prestige a soft and appealing look. Prestige is made from a viscose rayon and polyester blend and has a soft, supple hand making it perfect for drapery.

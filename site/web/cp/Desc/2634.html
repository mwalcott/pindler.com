2634-Cabriole is a Signature Exclusive upholstery chenille stripe. Cabriole is a beautifully textural upholstery railroaded stripe that blends bamboo rayon chenille rib stripes with lustrious rayon novelty yarns for an interesting play of surface dimension and texture. The variegated, unbalanced stripe makes this pattern a fresh and modern choice for a variety of upholstery applications.

4583-Salem is a multipurpose linen stripe. Salem is a traditional up the roll balanced stripe that has the French Laundry look and is woven in 100% Belgian linen. Salem is washed to make the fabric softer and more casual looking. Belgian Linen is known for its superior quality due to centuries-old farming, spinning, weaving and finishing techniques. Belgian linen is grown and produced in the best climatic regions for flax and linen yarns.

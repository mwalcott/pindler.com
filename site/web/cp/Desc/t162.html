T162-Carreau is an indoor/outdoor lipcord. Carreau is a 1/2" textural twist cord constructed in 100% Sunbrella solution dyed all-weather acrylic. Carreau is a twist cord that has alternating cut and uncut texture, that is attached to a lip making it easy for application. Carreau is used for embellishing pillows, upholstery, drapery, and accessories, both indoors and out.

2034-Sabella is a Signature Exclusive upholstery washed damask design. Sabella is a midscale classic Mediterranean tile design. Sabella is a traditional pattern of interlocked circles with decorated with Moorish paisleys and diamond rosettes. Sabella has a fantastic color vibrancy in each of the six colors. Sabella is woven in a rayon, cotton, and polyester blend and has been washed to enhance the look and feel of the fabric.

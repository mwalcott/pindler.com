3734-Windgate is an upholstery linen solid. Windgate is a classic twill pattern that has been woven using luxurious 100% Belgian linen. Belgian Linen is considered some of the highest quality linen available in the marketplace. To be Certified Belgian Linen, the fabric must go through the strictest of quality tests and requirements from the areas where the linen is grown, to the way it is spun, dyed, woven and finished. As a result, only premium quality linen passes such requirements. Windgate is woven with heavy spun yarns both in the warp and weft, and has a unique texture and depth with surface dimension. Windgate has a soft hand as well as a fabulous luster, hand and drape.

3977-Maduri is a Signature Exclusive upholstery small repeat. Maduri is a Kuba cloth inspired design that can function as a small repeat pattern or as a textural plain, depending on the use. Maduri is woven in a textural construction with a novelty shoelace yarn, which gives surface dimension and texture to the face of the fabric. Made in America using the finest yarns and fibers, this is a fabulous grouping of luxurious upholstery designs colored in sophisticated, modern metal neutrals accented with trend pop colors.

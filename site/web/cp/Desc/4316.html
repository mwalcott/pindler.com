4316-Rayure is a Signature Exclusive Richard Frinier indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® upholstery stripe. Rayure is an indoor/outdoor textural railroaded balanced stripe with a French Laundry look. Rayure is woven in a blend of Sunbrella solution dyed all-weather acrylic and recycled Sunbrella acrylic. These Signature Exclusive patterns are engineered with variances in the weave for a handwoven, organic look.

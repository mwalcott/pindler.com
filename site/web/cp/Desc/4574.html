4574-Verano is an antique slubbed upholstery solid velvet. Verano is woven with 100% Draylon Acrylic to achieve a sophisticated dry luster to emulate the look of a linen velvet. Draylon is a high quality acrylic fiber manufactured in Germany. Verano has undergone a special finishing process to achieve a vintage antique appearance. This finish adds nuances of shading and texture which adds to its unique modeled antiqued pile.

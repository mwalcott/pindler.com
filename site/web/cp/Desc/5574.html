5574-Belgique is an upholstery solid linen velvet. Belgique linen velvet is an old world classic with a modern day aesthetic. Belgique linen velvet is esteemed for its unique texture, luxurious hand, casual elegance, and long lasting wearability. Belgique linen velvet will develop its own patina with use to become more beautiful with age, this patina finish is distinctive to linen velvets with its subtle luster and dry matte antique quality. Belique can be used in both modern and old world settings.

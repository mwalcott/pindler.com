4562-Majorca is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® midscale geometric pattern. Majorca is a transitional midscale geometric fretwork design. Majorca is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

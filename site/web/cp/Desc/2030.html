2030-Meridian is an Exclusive upholstery contemporary stripe design. Meridian is a railroaded midscale band stripe design with a textural basketweave ground accented with two fresh, bright stripe colors. Meridian has an interesting background weave with natural flax and cotton, woven with rayon and polyester for a unique blend of yarn texture and shine. The lustrous stripes are bold and brilliant, with a herringbone texture pattern within the stripes for added surface interest. Meridian has been finished with Teflon for added strength and durability.

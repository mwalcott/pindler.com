4559-Kendari is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® midscale geometric. Kendari is a transitional ogee geometric design with a textural neutral colored ground with a contrasting figure in bright, clear colors. Kendari is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

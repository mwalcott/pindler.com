8996-Escalera is a Signature Exclusive Hearst Castle Indoor/Outdoor design. Escalera was inspired by Cuenca tiles used in a terrace stairway leading to Casa Grande. The use of ceramic tiles on the interior and exterior was an important feature in the Hearst Castle’s Mediterranean Revival style of architecture. Escalera is woven with intricate weave details and chenille accents. Escalera has been woven with Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all-weather acrylic yarns.

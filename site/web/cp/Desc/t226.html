T226-Chandon is a beaded tassel fringe trim. Chandon is a classic 5/8" flat grosgrain tape woven with viscose rayon and acrylic, with abalone shells and natural beads hand-sewn onto the edge. Chandon has a delicate and natural, yet sophisticated look. Chandon coordinates with T225- Camino, which is the grosgrain tape. Chandon is used for enhancing pillows, upholstery, bedding, drapery, and accessories.

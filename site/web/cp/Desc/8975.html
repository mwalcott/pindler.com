8975-Dominique is a Signature Exclusive upholstery washed damask design. Adapted from an 18th Century French Lyons document, Dominique has been exquisitely rendered and woven to capture an old world timelessness and sophistication. This luxurious design has been woven with double beam warps and incorporates several weave textures and ombreeing yarn colors. Dominique has been washed to enhance its rich texture, antique quality, and soft, comfortable hand.

3812-Zulu is a Signature Exclusive upholstery ethnic design. Zulu is a traditional Kente cloth pattern made modern by the use of interesting color combinations. Woven with textural boucle in the figure, Zulu has several different weave textures and colors within the pattern. Zulu is woven in a polyester, cotton, and rayon blend and meets 30,000 double rubs. Zulu is finished with Teflon.

1400-Grimaldi is a Signature Exclusive upholstery solid chenille design. Grimaldi is an eco-friendly solid chenille design that incorporates plush chenille yarns and novelty lash yarns for a balanced yet fun tonal fabric. Grimaldi is woven from a Rayon and Bamboo blend (78% Rayon & 22% Bamboo). Grimaldi coordinates with all the designs in our Luxury Textures Collection.

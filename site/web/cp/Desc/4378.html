4378-Truscott is a Signature Exclusive contemporary upholstery chenille stripe. Truscott is an ombre midscale stripe woven with different colors of chenille and a luster shoelace yarn for an interesting play of texture and weave effects. Luxury Textures are woven with the finest quality and innovative yarns spun from bamboos, cottons, linens, rayons which are woven into plush chenilles.

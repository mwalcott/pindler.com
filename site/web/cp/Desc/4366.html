4366-Tyrone is a Multipurpose solid Irish linen. Tyrone is a subtle, durable, and natural solid woven from 100% Irish linen and has a soil and stain resistant finish. Tyrone is a timeless group of solids that gives great versatility for several design applications. Linen fibers are elegant, understated, and will dye beautifully to an infinite spectrum of colors, deep rich, vibrant, or dense muted earth tones.

4958-Tularosa is an Exclusive upholstery large scale ethnic pattern. Tularosa is a classic Southwest diamond design in a rustic, natural looking construction with a bit of flax in it. Tularosa emulates hand woven horse blankets and Southwest carpets with the use of heavier novelty texture yarns and classic colorways. Tularosa works in today’s modern lodge looks, classic mountain cabins, or as a decorative Southwest accent to any room scene.

4554-Kasumi is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® matelasse. Kasumi is a transitional small scale pattern that looks like stitching in two directions, forming a check pattern. Kasumi is woven in a matelasse construction with texture and surface dimension. Kasumi is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

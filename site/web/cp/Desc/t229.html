T229-Chalfort is a jacquard tape trim. Chalfort is a modern geometric tape that is jacquard-woven in a diamond trellis design. Chalfort is a 2 5/8" jacquard tape woven in linen, viscose rayon, and acrylic. Chalfort is simple and classic, and has a subtle sophistication. Chalfort is used for embellishing pillows, upholstery, bedding, drapery, and accessories.

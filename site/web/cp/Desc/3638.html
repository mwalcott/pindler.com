3638-Chet is an exclusive upholstery small repeat design. Chet is a classic small scale diamond textural pattern done in greys and neutrals mixed with accent pop colors and woven with cotton, rayon, polyester and flax and comes with a soil and stain resistant finish. The patterns in Gentlemen’s Retreat are exclusive to Pindler & Pindler and have been colored in classic sophisticated men’s suiting palettes paired with eye catching accent pop colors.

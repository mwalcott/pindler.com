9806-Clark is a Signature Exclusive upholstery Sunbrella indoor-outdoor stripe design. Clark is a classic and interesting unbalanced stripe pattern which is constructed with Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all-weather acrylic yarns. This special acrylic fiber is virtually impervious to moisture damage, is mold and mildew resistant, cleans easily, and is resistant to fading, even when exposed to the elements.

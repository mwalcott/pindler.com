2668-Greek Key is a Signature Exclusive upholstery contemporary geometric design. Greek Key is an elegant but edgy textural geometric pattern woven in a fabulously textural construction. Greek Key uses a variety of different yarns, including novelty, boucles, textural linen yarns, and twisted blend yarns to give an organic earthiness to the surface. Greek Key is a woven in a cotton, rayon, linen and polyester blend and is finished with Teflon.

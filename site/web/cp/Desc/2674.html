2674-Serafina is a Signature Exclusive upholstery contemporary damask design. Serafina is a large-scale lattice fretwork pattern that incorporates a honeycomb-like deflected double weave ground with the figure woven in twill, which seems to sit on the surface. Serafina is a gorgeous three-dimensional design that is woven in a rayon, cotton, and polyester blend and is finished with Teflon.

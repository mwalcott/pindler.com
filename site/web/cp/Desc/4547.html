4547-Bayeux is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® large scale pattern. Bayeux is a stylized tie dye pattern, with different colors and weaves forming a tie dyed look. Bayeux is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

T191-Byblos is a beautiful 2 3/4" tassel fringe. Byblos has a header that is made up of a stripe grosgrain woven in a lustrous vicscose rayon. Hanging from the header are tassels that have been hand tied with a lustrous novelty yarn. Byblos is an elegant trim that enhances bedding, draperies, upholstery, and accessories.

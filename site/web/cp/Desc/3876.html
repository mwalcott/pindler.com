3876-Newgate is a Signature Exclusive Sunbrella indoor/outdoor upholstery pattern. Newgate is a transitional geometric fretwork design that is woven in 100% Sunbrella all-weather solution dyed acrylic. Newgate is woven with a small denier Sunbrella chenille that has a lot of texture and loft. Newgate is a full faced chenille that appears two-toned with different weave effects that form the drawing.

3633-Henley is an exclusive upholstery stripe design. Henley is a midscale scallop stripe design that features an accent of color forming an outline on the scallop edges. Henley can be used in an eclectic/ethnic way or in a modern way. Henley is woven in a beautiful rayon, cotton, flax and polyester blend and comes with a soil and stain resistant finish. The patterns in Gentlemen’s Retreat are exclusive to Pindler & Pindler and have been colored in classic sophisticated men’s suiting palettes paired with eye catching accent pop colors.

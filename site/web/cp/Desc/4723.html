4723-Walker is an Exclusive upholstery chenille stripe. Walker is a classic two color balanced stripe with 2" stripes of chenille alternating with 2" stripes of texture yarns for a good "go-with" for any of the patterns in this collection. This collection is an authentic Southwestern grouping that gets its inspiration from classic design icons of the American Indian culture. Known for their use of simple geometry of symbolic design, much of their textiles incorporated the art of balance within nature.

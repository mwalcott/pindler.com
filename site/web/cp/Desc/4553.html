4553-Caldwell is an upholstery indoor/outdoor Sunbrella® herringbone. Caldwell is a classic herringbone texture that is woven with a novelty twist yarn for added surface dimension. Caldwell is woven in Sunbrella 100% solution dyed all weather acrylic and is mildew, fade, soil and stain resistant. It is a great fabric for the house, on the deck, on the water, or by the pool.

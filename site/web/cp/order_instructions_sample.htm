INSTRUCTIONS

1.

Enter the pattern # then click the "Find Pattern Number"  link.  When the pattern number and name fields populate, fill in all required fields, i.e., size, desired color, and  sidemark.  
OR

2.

Enter the entire pattern name then click the "Find Pattern Name" link.  When the pattern name and number fields populate, fill in all required fields, i.e., size, desired color, and  sidemark.  
OR

3.

Enter the first 3-letters of the pattern name ONLY  then click the "Find Pattern Name" link.  The pattern name field will turn into a drop-down box.  Expand the box and select the desired pattern name.  Fill in all required fields, i.e., size, desired color, and  sidemark.  

To delete a line item, click  the "Delete Item" button in the right column.  If you have not completed a line item, and you want to start over, click the Clear icon. If you are not ready to submit your order, click the Save Order button before navigating away from the window. Your information will remain in tact the next time you log on. When you are finished adding items, click the "Submit Order" button.  If you are satisfied with the order, click the "Final Submit" button.  If you are not satisfied, click the "Modify Order" button.   You will receive a confirmation of your order via email.  Please allow 24-hours for processing.  If you have any questions, please contact our order desk at 800-669-6002, Monday thru Friday from 6:00 a.m. PST to 5:00 p.m. PST.
